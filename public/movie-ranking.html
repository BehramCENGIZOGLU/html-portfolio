The Best Movies According to Behram

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Godfather

The Godfather is a powerful crime drama that follows the transformation of Michael Corleone from reluctant outsider to ruthless mafia boss.

Fight Club

Fight Club is a dark psychological drama about an insomniac office worker who forms an underground fight club that spirals into chaos and self-destruction.

Fast and Furious series

Fast and Furious is a high-octane action film series that centers on illegal street racing, heists, and the unbreakable bond of family.
